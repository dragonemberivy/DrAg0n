COOK'S GUIDE 🍲

  • Raw Meat / Bone Shards: Culled from wild fauna or livestock.
  • Sweet Berries / Wild Yeast: Harvested randomly from standard flora.
  • Cave Salts / Glowing Moss: Mined from stalactites deep underground.
  • Toxic Fungus: Found exclusively on Toxic biomes.
  • Spicy Bulbs: Found exclusively on Inferno biomes.
  • Crystalized Sap: Found exclusively on Frozen biomes.
  • Carbon / Nanites: Basic resource harvesting & Sentinel combat.
  • Tip: Build a Nutrient Processor (Build Menu 6) to combine these into powerful meals!
